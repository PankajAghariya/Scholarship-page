Our Scholarship Programs

We offer various scholarship opportunities to support students at different stages of their educational journey.

Merit-Based Scholarship

For students with exceptional academic performance and a passion for learning. Covers tuition fees and educational materials.

  • Full tuition coverage
  • Study materials included
  • Mentorship program
Up to ₹50,000/year Application Deadline: March 31, 2025

Need-Based Scholarship

Designed to support students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who demonstrate potential and determination.

  • Financial aid support
  • Educational resources
  • Career counseling
Up to ₹75,000/year Application Deadline: March 31, 2025

Graduate Studies Scholarship

For students pursuing higher education, research, or specialized courses to advance their career goals.

  • Advanced degree support
  • Research funding
  • Professional development
Up to ₹1,00,000/year Application Deadline: April 30, 2025

Eligibility Criteria

General requirements for all scholarship programs

01

Academic Excellence

Minimum 75% marks in previous academic year or equivalent CGPA

02

Financial Need

Demonstrated financial need with supporting documentation

03

Community Involvement

Active participation in community service or social initiatives

04

Indian Citizenship

Must be a citizen of India or have valid residency status

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for our scholarship programs

1

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application with all required details and documents

2

Document Submission

Upload necessary documents including academic records, income certificates, and recommendation letters

3

Review & Screening

4

5

Recipients will be notified via email and phone call within 4-6 weeks

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our scholarship programs

When is the application deadline?

+

Application deadlines vary by scholarship type. Merit-based and Need-based scholarships have a deadline of March 31, 2025, while Graduate Studies scholarships have a deadline of April 30, 2025.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

+

Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarship programs, but you can only receive one scholarship at a time. Please indicate your preference in the application form.

How are scholarship recipients selected?

+

Recipients are selected based on academic performance, financial need, community involvement, and the quality of their statement of purpose. Our committee reviews all applications thoroughly and fairly.

Is the scholarship renewable?

+

Yes, scholarships are renewable annually based on maintaining minimum academic performance (75% or equivalent) and continued financial need. Recipients must reapply each year.

When will I know if I've been selected?

+

All applicants will be notified of the decision within 4-6 weeks after the application deadline. Selected candidates will receive an email and phone call.
